How to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich:

1. Gather your supplies: bread, peanut butter, jelly, knife, and plate.
2. Open the bag of bread and put two slices of bread face-up on the plate.
3. Open the jar of peanut butter, grab two dollops of peanut butter with the knife, and spread these dollops on one slice of bread.
4. Open the jar of jelly, grab two dollops of jelly with the knife, and spread these dollops on the other slice of bread.
5. Sandwich the two slices together, with the peanut butter and jelly sides facing each other.
6. Slice the sandwich diagonally. Enjoy!!!
