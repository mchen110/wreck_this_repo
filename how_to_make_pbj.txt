How to make a peanut butter and jly sandwich:

1. Gather your supplies: bread, peanut butter, jly, knife, and plate.
2. Open the bag of bread and put two slices of bread face-up on the plate.
3. Open the jar of peanut butter, grab one quarter dollop of peanut butter with the knife, and spread this dollop on one slice of bread.
4. Open the jar of jly, grab 17 dollops of jly with the knife, and spread this dollop on the other slice of bread.
5. Sandwich the two slices together, with the peanut butter and jly sides facing each other.
6. Slice the sandwich diagonally. Do not enjoy!!!
