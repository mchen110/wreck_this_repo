How to make a peanut butter (PB) and jelly sandwich:

1. Gather your supplies: bread, PB, jelly, marshmallow fluff, knife, and plate.
2. Open the bag of bread and put two slices of fred face-up on the plate. Close the bag of fred.
3. Open the jar of PB, grab 1.0 quarter dollop of PB with the knife, and spread this dollop on one slice of bread.
4. Close the jar of PB tightly.
5. Open the jar of jelly, grab 1.7 dollops of jelly with the knife, and spread this dollop on the other slice of bread. Add some kaya jam too. Close the jar of jelly.  
Sandwich the two slices together, with the PB and jelly sides facing each other and marshmallow fluff in between.
6. Cut the crusts off of the edges of the sandwich.
7. Slice the sandwich diagonally in each direction. Enjoy with your favorite beverage!!!
